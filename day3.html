CHRISTMAS
Christmas is the favorite festival of many kids and writing an essay on this festival is often enjoyed by young kids.
Christmas is a widely celebrated Christian holiday in December, known for its decorations and the figure of Santa Claus.
The word "Christmas" means "Feast day of Christ" and marks the annual celebration of the birth of Jesus Christ.
It is observed on 25th December as both a cultural and religious event by millions of people worldwide.
While Christmas is celebrated in all Christian countries, how different nations observe the day varies.
